About Us

Himalaya Network Group

A legacy of excellence in Nepal-Japan business relations since 1997

Himalaya Network Group of Companies was established under the leadership of Mr. Surendra Shakya, is a multipurpose company with a profound commitment to promoting Nepal’s rich heritage, tourism, and trade globally. From its head office at Shree Durbar, Pulchowk, Lalitpur, Nepal, Himalaya Network stands as a beacon of excellence, bringing together a portfolio of businesses dedicated to handicrafts, international trade, tourism, and humanitarian initiatives.

The company, registered in Nepal under Registration No. 23441/059/60 with an authorized capital of NPR 48,000,000, has been serving both domestic and international markets for decades. Himalaya Network has always believed in the philosophy of combining commerce with compassion, driving both economic growth and social development.
