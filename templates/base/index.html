About Us

Himalaya Network Group

A legacy of excellence in Nepal-Japan business relations since 1997

Himalaya Network Group of Companies was founded in 1997 with a vision to bridge the gap between Nepal and Japan in trade, tourism, and cultural exchange. What started as a small trading company has grown into a diversified conglomerate with five specialized companies.

Our journey began with exporting traditional Nepali handicrafts to Japan, and over the years, we have expanded our horizons to include tourism services, IT solutions, real estate, and more.
