Our Trade Projects

Bridging Nepal and Japan through trade exhibitions and cultural exchange

2004

Pacific Flora

Hamamatsu, Shizuoka, Japan

The Shizuoka International Garden and Horticulture Exhibition "Pacific Flora 2004" is the third time the international horticultural exhibition has been held in Japan, recognized by the International Association of Horticultural Producers (AIPH). The theme is "Flowers, Greenery, Water: Creating New Style of Living. We at the Himalayan Network are planning the NEPAL GARDEN, which will include a precise recreation of a traditional Nepalese garden, to include a gift shop and restaurant serving authentic Nepalese food. For more information, please see the official Expo website.

Our participation in Pacific Flora 2004 helped establish lasting business relationships with Japanese importers and distributors. The exhibition provided a platform to introduce Nepal's unique products to a sophisticated market, leading to successful export deals that continue to benefit Nepali artisans and farmers.

Key Highlights

  • Over 10,000 visitors attended
  • Established 25+ business partnerships
  • Featured traditional Nepali handicrafts, pashmina, and organic products
2005

Fukuoka Fair

Fukuoka, Japan

The Fukuoka Fair 2005 built upon the success of Pacific Flora, expanding Nepal's presence in the Japanese market. This comprehensive trade exhibition focused on 'Life Cycle of Lord Buddha in a Flower Garden'.

Our delegation showcased Nepal's rich cultural heritage, adventure tourism opportunities, and investment potential. The fair featured live demonstrations of traditional Nepali crafts, cultural performances, and business matchmaking sessions that connected Nepali entrepreneurs with Japanese investors.

Key Highlights

  • Promoted Nepal as a premier tourism destination
  • Featured investment opportunities in Nepal
  • Cultural performances showcasing Nepali traditions
  • Business forums connecting 100+ Japanese companies
